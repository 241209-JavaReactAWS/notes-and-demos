Grilled Salmon Recipe

Ingredients

  • Slab of salmon
  • Avocado Oil
  • Your favorite seasonings
    • salt
    • pepper
    • paprika

Cooking Instructions

  1. On a cutting board you can clean easily, slice salmon slab into 8oz filets
  2. Preheat grill to 400 degrees
  3. Lightly drizzle oil onto the salmon
  4. Add you favorite seasonings
  5. Grill for 12 minutes and flip after 8
